// 01 — E-COMMERCE
What Drives Purchasing Behaviour in E-Commerce

A full analysis pipeline — Excel to MySQL to Power BI — built on 9,994 real e-commerce orders, uncovering exactly where a business was quietly losing money.

Orders with no discount return a healthy 29.5% profit margin. Push the discount past 40%, and margin collapses to -77.4% — meaning the business loses money on every single one of those 933 orders.

// 02 — RETAIL
Retail Inventory Risk & Demand Forecasting

A Python + Power BI analysis of 2 years of daily sales across 5 stores and 20 products — answering two operational questions: which products are about to run out, and how far ahead can demand actually be forecast?

Nearly half of all store-product combinations are already below their reorder point — and the risk isn't concentrated in one category or region, it's systemic.

  • Weekly demand forecasts are far more reliable than daily ones — daily noise cancels out at the weekly level.
  • Discounting has no measurable effect on sales volume here — a deliberate contrast with the e-commerce project, showing discount impact is business-specific, not universal.
  • Holiday and weather effects exist but are too small to act on independently.
